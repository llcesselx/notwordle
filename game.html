Not Wordle

Not Wordle is not Wordle but has the same rules as Wordle. You, the player, have six guesses to figure out the secret word. If you cannot guess it after six tries, you lose. You will guess a five letter word and if the letter is in the word, the letter will turn yellow. If the letter is in the word AND in the correct place, the letter will turn green. Try and guess the right word!
